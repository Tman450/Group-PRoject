History

Three epicurious students traveled around the globe seeking high quality burgers to serve at a lower price to the general populace. After a vast array of visits, they have reached an upper echelon and established the fast food restaurant The Summit Burger.

Established in 2018, we serve burgers, fries, and gluten-free diet water. We target unique and loyal customers. Our water is sourced from the French Alps. Our fries are made in the richest environment of Germany. Our beef is sourced from northern California.

Those of us at The Summit Burger are committed to quality and excellence as our journey proves. We strive to provide the highest quality burger experience to the largest amount of customers.

We have made sure our burgers are:

  • Juicy
  • Fresh
  • Thick
  • Easy To Eat
